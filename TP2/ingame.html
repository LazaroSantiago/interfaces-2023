How to play

Players take turns dropping one of their colored discs into any of the seven columns. The disc will fall to the lowest unoccupied space in the selected column.

The game continues with players taking alternating turns.

The objective is to be the first to connect four of your own discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The row can be in any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

If the grid becomes completely filled with discs, and no player has four in a row, the game ends in a draw.

Controls

You play by using your touchscreen or mouse to select, drag, and drop coins into columns on the game grid. You can tap/click to pick up a coin, drag it to the desired column, and release to drop it. The game checks move validity, monitors winning conditions, and may offer optional features like undo/redo, restart, and settings adjustments for an enhanced gaming experience.
